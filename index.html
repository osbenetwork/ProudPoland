POLAND FIRST

Poland chose sovereignty and safety.
It works.

By rejecting EU migrant relocation schemes and maintaining strict border control, Poland has preserved its cultural identity, public safety, and national cohesion.

🇵🇱

Firm Rejection of Mass Immigration

Poland has consistently refused mandatory EU migrant quotas. In 2025, the government secured a full opt-out from relocation mechanisms and related financial contributions.

🛡️

One of Europe's Safest Countries

Extremely low homicide rate, minimal violent crime, and virtually no terrorism. Cities like Warsaw and Kraków remain welcoming and secure for families and visitors alike.

📈

Economy & Culture for Poles

Focus on Polish workers, controlled and selective legal migration when needed, and strong social cohesion. No parallel societies or no-go zones.

PROVEN RESULTS • MARCH 2026

What strong borders deliver

🔒

Secure Eastern Border

Physical barriers, advanced surveillance, and continued buffer zones have dramatically reduced illegal crossing attempts. Poland maintains control despite hybrid pressures from Belarus.

📉

Doubled Deportations

In 2025, Poland carried out over 2,100 forced deportations — nearly double the previous year — sending a clear message that illegal stay will not be tolerated.

🕊️

Exceptional Safety

Among the lowest crime rates in the EU. One of the safest countries according to the Global Peace Index. No major terrorist incidents and high public trust in institutions.

🏛️

Cultural Cohesion

With a very low percentage of non-European foreign-born residents, Poland preserves its Christian heritage, traditions, and social harmony without the tensions seen elsewhere.

💪

Sovereignty Upheld

Poland continues to prioritize national interests over EU migration pacts. Recent opt-outs confirm that protecting Polish citizens comes first.

📊

Economic Strength

Growth benefits Polish families. Low unemployment among citizens and a selective approach to labor migration ensure resources serve the nation first.

DEFENDING THE NATION

Strong borders = Real security

Poland has invested heavily in physical and technological border protection. The eastern frontier remains tightly controlled, with ongoing extensions of buffer zones and surveillance systems.

✅

Dramatic reduction in successful illegal crossings

Thanks to barriers, sensors, and rapid response teams.

✅

East Shield program expanding

Further fortification of over 200 km of eastern border in 2026.

✅

Pushback policy maintained

Protecting sovereignty against hybrid threats.
